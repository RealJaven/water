Helpdesk/FAQ

Q: What is UniThirstQ?
A: UniThirstQ is a web system that will be used by all kinds of target users (students, staff, visitors, passersby) on the USYD campus to help them find and navigate to a facility with drinking water. 

Q: How do I use this website?
A: You can find nearby bubblers, nearby vending machines / cafés / restaurants and all drinking facilities by distance, and display them in the list and the embeded map. In addition, there will be an option to download a campus map with labeled drinking facilities. If you would to write reviews, you will need to sign in first. Also, you can redirect to GoogleMap.

Q: How do I use Google Map
A: When you click the button of a drinking facility, there will an option to redirect to GoogleMap.
